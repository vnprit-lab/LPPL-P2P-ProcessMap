DoA Approval Workflow
System-based approvals. Physical PO signing not required post initial approval. Annualized limits per vendor per item. Approval mail attached to SAP.
Class A: >5 Cr → COO+CFO Class B: 1-5 Cr → COO Class C: <1 Cr → FC

Non-negotiable TAT. Goods must clear port/CFS within 48 hours of duty clearance. Transport pre-scheduled before duty payment. Warehouse confirms storage readiness.

Phase 07
Warehouse & Stores Control
24-hour GRN rule. QC inspection before production release. Manual Security Register + digital movement report, synced weekly with SCM. Periodic physical verification by cross-functional team.
Stores Head GRN: 24 hrs SAP B1 GRN
Stores Gates
GRN EntryWithin 24 hours of arrival
Production IssueOnly after GRN + QC pass
Physical AuditCross-functional team → FC + COO
⚠ Control Gate 3
Mandatory 4-Way Match
System-driven hard-block. No payment without: Approved PO + Finalized GRN + QC Inspection Report + Vendor Invoice. Failed match requires original PO approver sign-off.
HARD BLOCK AP Head verifies Rejection: 1-day TAT
The Four Pillars
Pillar 1Approved PO (price + tags + terms)
Pillar 2Finalized GRN (physical receipt)
Pillar 3QC Inspection Report (BESS standards)
Pillar 4Vendor Invoice (qty + price match)
Phase 08
Payment Mechanism
Bi-weekly payment rhythm (Tue/Thu). AP Head extracts Ready-to-Pay list from SAP. SCM reviews for quality disputes. FC gives final sign-off. Statutory payments prioritized.
AP Head FC approves Bi-weekly cycle
Payment Controls

New Vendor AdvanceMax 5% (<6 months history)

BG/SecurityMandatory for all advances
Phase 09
LC Three-Key Process
Treasury drafts LC from approved PO. FC vets financial risk. SCM Head validates delivery alignment. Vendor reviews and accepts. Formal issuance within 5 working days.
Treasury FC SCM Head 5 working days
